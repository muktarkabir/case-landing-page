What is CASE?

The CASE platform is a centralized digital hub that connects individuals with a broad range of support organizations, making it easier for people to access the help they need. By bringing diverse support services together in one place, we remove the traditional barriers that hinder timely assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who should use CASE?

Anyone seeking help (food, shelter, legal aid, counseling, etc.) and verified support organizations that want to respond to those needs.

How do I get help?

You look for a support organization by using the search feature, the search is made easy by various search filters including organization category, location etc. You then initiate a chat with your preferred support organization, marking the start of your case.

How do donations work?

CASE does not process money, public stories include links to external donation pages so funds go directly to the organization named in the post.

Are organizations verified and how do I trust them?

Organizations go through lightweight verification during onboarding (documents, links, community references) and display availability and profile details so you can choose who to contact.
